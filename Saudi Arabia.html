About Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is a large country located in the Middle East, occupying most of the Arabian Peninsula. Its capital city is Riyadh, a modern and fast-growing city known for its skyscrapers and business centers. The official currency of Saudi Arabia is the Saudi Riyal (SAR). The country is famous for its rich history, Islamic heritage, and holy cities Makkah and Madinah, which attract millions of pilgrims every year. Saudi Arabia also has vast deserts, beautiful mountains, and modern cities like Jeddah and Dammam. Today, it plays an important role in the world’s economy and culture.

Famous Places

Saudi Arabia is a country rich in history, culture, and religion. The most famous and sacred place in the country is the Kaaba, located in the Holy Mosque (Masjid Al-Haram) in Makkah. It is the holiest site in Islam, and millions of Muslims from all over the world visit it every year to perform Hajj and Umrah. The Kaaba is a large cube-shaped building covered with a black cloth called the Kiswah, and it is the direction Muslims face during prayer.
Another important city is Madinah, home to the Prophet’s Mosque, where Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) is buried. Outside the holy cities, Saudi Arabia also has amazing modern attractions like Riyadh’s Kingdom Tower, Jeddah Corniche, and the historical site of Al-Ula. These places show the beauty of Saudi Arabia’s mix of tradition, spirituality, and modern development.

Tourism

Saudi Arabia is a country rich in culture, history, and natural beauty, making it an attractive destination for tourists. The capital, Riyadh, offers modern attractions, shopping malls, and historical sites like Masmak Fort. Jeddah, located on the Red Sea, is famous for its beautiful Corniche, beaches, and the historic Al-Balad district. Saudi Arabia is also home to religious tourism, with millions visiting Mecca and Medina each year. Nature lovers can explore the stunning deserts of Rub’ al Khali and the mountains of Asir. The country is opening more to international tourism, offering a mix of tradition, adventure, and luxury experiences.
